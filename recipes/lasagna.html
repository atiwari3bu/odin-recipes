Lasagna

How to make Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that combines layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese into a delectable and comforting casserole. To prepare this mouthwatering lasagna, start by browning ground beef or Italian sausage in a large skillet over medium heat. Once cooked, add diced onions, minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant. Pour in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and a splash of red wine for added depth. Season the sauce with dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to taste, and let it simmer to allow the flavors to meld.

In a separate pot, prepare the creamy béchamel sauce by melting butter and stirring in flour to create a roux. Gradually whisk in milk, stirring until the sauce thickens. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper for a delightful hint of flavor. To assemble the lasagna, layer cooked lasagna noodles in a baking dish, followed by the meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and a generous sprinkle of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, finishing with a layer of cheese on top. Bake the lasagna in the oven until the cheese is golden and bubbly, and serve hot, letting the layers of savory goodness meld together in every bite.

Ingredients Required

    For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Make

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
